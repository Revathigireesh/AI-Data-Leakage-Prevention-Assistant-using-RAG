COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT
=======================

LEAVE POLICY
------------
Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves and 12 sick leaves per year.
Casual leaves can be applied for personal reasons with at least 1 day prior notice.
Sick leaves require a medical certificate if taken for more than 2 consecutive days.
Annual leaves (earned leaves) accrue at 1.5 days per month, totaling 18 days per year.
Unused casual leaves cannot be carried forward to the next year.
Unused annual (earned) leaves can be carried forward, up to a maximum of 30 days.
Maternity leave is available for 26 weeks for female employees as per government regulations.
Paternity leave is available for 5 working days within 6 months of the child's birth.
Leave applications must be submitted through the HR portal at least 2 days in advance for planned leaves.
Emergency leaves can be applied retrospectively within 2 days of returning to work.
National and state public holidays are observed as per the official government calendar.
The list of public holidays for the year is shared by HR at the start of each year.

WORK FROM HOME (WFH) POLICY
----------------------------
Employees are allowed up to 8 work-from-home days per month.
WFH is not permitted on Mondays and Fridays unless approved by the reporting manager.
New employees (less than 6 months tenure) are required to work from office full-time.
WFH requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 1 day in advance.
Employees on WFH are expected to be available on calls and respond within 30 minutes.
Temporary relocation for work (working from a different city) must be approved by HR and the department head.
Temporary remote work from another city is allowed for a maximum of 30 days per year.
Employees must ensure a stable internet connection and quiet workspace while on WFH.

OFFICE TIMINGS
--------------
Standard office hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.
The office is closed on weekends (Saturday and Sunday).
Flexible timing is available: employees may arrive between 8:00 AM and 11:00 AM and complete 9 hours of work.
Core hours (when all employees must be available) are 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
Lunch break is 30 minutes and is included in the 9-hour workday.
The office premises are accessible from 7:30 AM to 9:00 PM on working days.
Overtime must be approved in advance by the reporting manager.

TRAVEL AND REIMBURSEMENT
-------------------------
Business travel expenses are reimbursed within 15 working days of submission.
Reimbursement claims must be submitted with original receipts or invoices.
Flight bookings above economy class require approval from the department head.
Local travel for business (cab, auto) is reimbursed as per actuals up to Rs. 1500 per day.
Hotel stays for business travel are covered up to Rs. 4000 per night in metro cities.
Reimbursement forms must be submitted through the finance portal within 30 days of the expense.

ONBOARDING AND BACKGROUND VERIFICATION
----------------------------------------
All new employees must submit original educational certificates for verification.
Background verification (BGV) includes employment history, education, and address checks.
BGV is conducted by a third-party agency and typically takes 15 to 21 working days.
Employees must submit a valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, passport, or driving licence) during onboarding.
PAN card details are required for payroll and tax purposes.
Bank account details must be provided within the first week for salary processing.
Joining documents must be submitted on or before the date of joining.

DRESS CODE
----------
The office follows a business casual dress code on all weekdays.
Formal attire is expected when meeting clients or attending official events.
Casual wear (jeans, t-shirts) is permitted on Fridays.

HR HELPDESK
-----------
The HR helpdesk can be reached at hr@company.com or through the internal HR portal.
HR working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
For urgent queries, employees can call the HR hotline at extension 1000.
Payroll queries must be raised by the 5th of each month to reflect in the current month's salary.
